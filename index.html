Cyclical Carpet Initiative Program Overview

For the benefit of property management professionals, Occupied Flooring Solutions (OFS) is teaming with Boyd Watterson Asset Management LLC and Milliken Flooring to implement a National Cyclical Carpet Initiative. The teaming agreement will cover both material and installation. Material options include Milliken’s Remix Remastered and Free Flow collections.

Our initial consultation will identify the particulars of your project focusing on the timeframe, the facility and tenant requirements. Following the initial consultation, a site visit will be coordinated to provide an in-depth project review and implementation path tailored to your project’s specific needs.

You can expect the installation process to be non-disruptive to the tenants. On average, our production rate of 10,000 SF per week. All projects are “phased” and documented well in advance of each project’s start.
